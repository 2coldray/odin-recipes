Burger Recipe

Description

To make a burger, you need to cook the ground beef, apply toppings and enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Ground beef patties
  • Cheese
  • Hamburger buns
  • Toppings of your choice

Steps

  1. Season and cook your ground patties.
  2. Apply and Melt cheese on top after patties are done.
  3. Add whatever toppings you want and enjoy!
