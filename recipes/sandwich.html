Turkey Sandwich Recipe

Description

A turkey sandwich is a classic meal that can be just requires you to add your favorite bread, turkey, and toppings together.

Ingredients

  • Turkey
  • Bread
  • Cheese
  • Toppings

Steps

  1. Grab two slices of bread.
  2. Add one serving of turkey to the bread.
  3. Add cheese on top of the turkey.
  4. Add your toppings to the sandwich and enjoy!
