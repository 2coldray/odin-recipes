Pizza Recipe

Description

This beautiful picture above is of pizza. Add bread, sauce, and your favorite toppings, bake in the oven, and you're good to go!

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Mozella Cheese
  • Pepporini
  • Any other toppings of your likings.

Steps

  1. Spread Maranara Sauce on the bread.
  2. Layer the cheese on top of the maranara sauce.
  3. Add all of your toppings.
  4. Bake in the oven at 400 degrees for 20-30 minutes.
